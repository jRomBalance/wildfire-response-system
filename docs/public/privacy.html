Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2026  ·  Last Updated: July 18, 2026  ·  ROM Technology / WildfireNet

📵 We do not sell, rent, share, or trade your mobile phone number or personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

1. Who We Are

WildfireNet is a wildfire detection and alert service operated by ROM Technology ("we," "us," or "our"), accessible at romallen.com and wildfire.romallen.com. We provide real-time wildfire detection alerts via SMS text message and email to individuals who voluntarily opt in to receive them.

Contact: alerts@romallen.com

2. Information We Collect

When you sign up for WildfireNet alerts, we may collect:

  • Name — to personalize your alerts
  • Mobile phone number — to send SMS wildfire alerts
  • Email address — to send email wildfire alerts
  • Location / ZIP code / city — to send region-specific alerts relevant to you
  • Opt-in consent record — date, time, and method of consent (required by law)

We do not collect payment information, social security numbers, or sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Send you wildfire detection alerts, air quality warnings, and smoke event notifications for your region
  • Send you HELP responses when you text HELP to our number
  • Process your opt-out when you text STOP
  • Maintain records of your consent as required by TCPA and carrier regulations
  • Improve the accuracy and relevance of alerts we send

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to wildfire alerts.

4. SMS Messaging — Important Disclosures

📱 SMS Alert Program Disclosures

  • Program name: WildfireNet Alerts by ROM Technology
  • Message frequency: Varies based on fire and air quality activity in your region. During active wildfire events, you may receive multiple messages per day. During quiet periods, you may receive none.
  • Message & data rates may apply. Standard SMS and data rates from your mobile carrier apply to all messages received.
  • To opt out: Text STOP to any WildfireNet message at any time. You will receive one confirmation message and no further alerts.
  • For help: Text HELP to any WildfireNet message or email alerts@romallen.com
  • Supported carriers: AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and most US carriers. Carrier support may vary.
  • Mobile number sharing: Your mobile phone number is NEVER shared with third parties for marketing or any other purpose.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use Twilio (SMS delivery) and SendGrid (email delivery) to send alerts. These providers process your phone number and email solely to deliver messages on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Emergency situations: In a genuine life-safety emergency, we may share location information with emergency services.

We do not share your mobile phone number with any third party for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as you are subscribed to WildfireNet alerts. When you opt out (text STOP or request removal), we will delete your mobile number from our active alert list within 30 days. We retain opt-in/opt-out records for 4 years as required by TCPA compliance regulations.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Opt out of SMS: Text STOP to any WildfireNet message
  • Opt out of email: Click "Unsubscribe" in any alert email
  • Request your data: Email alerts@romallen.com to request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Request deletion: Email alerts@romallen.com to request deletion of your data
  • Update your information: Email alerts@romallen.com to update your phone number, email, or region

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. This includes encrypted data transmission, secure API key management, and access controls on our systems. However, no system is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

WildfireNet is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at alerts@romallen.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. Third-Party Services

Our alert system uses the following third-party services:

  • NASA FIRMS — Fire detection satellite data (no personal data shared)
  • EPA AirNow — Air quality data (no personal data shared)
  • Twilio — SMS message delivery (Twilio Privacy Policy)
  • SendGrid — Email delivery (SendGrid Privacy Policy)

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify active subscribers via SMS or email. Continued use of WildfireNet alerts after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
