How It Works

Five layers. One mission.

Every layer feeds the next - from space to the ground to your phone in minutes.

🛰
Layer 1

Satellite Detection

NASA FIRMS VIIRS sensors detect heat signatures at 375m resolution, updated every 10 minutes.

🌲
Layer 2

IoT Ground Sensors

Solar-powered sensors detect CO, VOC, and PM2.5 at the smoldering phase within 3 minutes of ignition.

🧠
Layer 3

AI Fusion Engine

Cross-validates satellite, sensor, and camera data. Eliminates false positives. Assigns severity score.

📡
Layer 4

Instant Alerts

SMS and email alerts go directly to firefighter units with GPS coordinates and severity.

🚁
Layer 5

Drone Response

Autonomous drones launch from solar hangars, confirm fire via infrared, and drop retardant.
